About Us

Akshar By KGK Realty is a residential project, located at Mansarovar Extension, Jaipur. It is a high-rise residential project having 3-4 BHK luxurious apartments. It comprises of 7 towers and a well equipped & modern club house. Each tower consists of 3 BHK with servant quarter & 4 BHK with servant quarter, ranging from 1382 Sq. Ft. to 2674 Sq. Ft. The project comfortably connects to all important places of the city without compromising its uniqueness.
